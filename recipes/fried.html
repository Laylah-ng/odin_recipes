Fried Rice recipe

How to prepare fried rice

Ingredients

  • vgetable oil
  • washed rice
  • onions
  • green onion
  • sweet corn
  • green peas
  • garlic
  • cloves garlic

Steps

  1. Ensure that your cooked rice is cooled and not too sticky. It's ideal to use leftover rice that has been refrigerated.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add chopped onions and minced garlic. Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until the onions are translucent.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and continue to stir-fry for an additional 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet, creating an empty space.
  6. Pour the beaten eggs into the empty space. Allow them to set for a moment and then scramble them with a spatula until cooked through.
  7. Mix the scrambled eggs with the vegetables in the skillet.
  8. Add the cooked and cooled rice to the skillet. Use a spatula to break up any clumps of rice and evenly distribute it among the vegetables and eggs.
  9. Drizzle soy sauce and oyster sauce over the rice mixture. Stir well to combine and evenly distribute the sauces.
  10. Drizzle sesame oil over the fried rice and stir to incorporate.
  11. Taste the fried rice and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
  12. Garnish the fried rice with chopped green onions for freshness.
  13. Serve the fried rice hot as a main dish or as a side dish. It pairs well with your favorite protein, such as chicken, shrimp, or tofu.

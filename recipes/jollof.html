Jollof recipes

how to cook tasty African jollof rice

Ingredients

  • washed rice
  • bell peppers
  • tomato paste
  • tatashe
  • tomato
  • salt
  • maggi
  • thyme
  • curry
  • onion

procedures

  1. Chop the onion, dice the tomatoes and bell peppers, and mince the garlic
  2. In a large pot or a deep skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onions and minced garlic to the hot oil. Saut√© until the onions are translucent.
  4. Add the blended or diced tomatoes and red bell pepper to the pot. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the mixture thickens.
  5. Add ground ginger, thyme, bay leaves, salt, and pepper to the tomato and pepper mixture. Stir well to combine.
  6. Pour in the rinsed rice and stir until it is well-coated with the tomato and spice mixture
  7. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low.Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  8. If you're adding protein (chicken, fish, shrimp, etc.), you can place it on top of the rice about 10-15 minutes before the rice is done. This allows the protein to cook along with the rice.
  9. Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork to separate the grains.
  10. Serve the Jollof rice hot, garnished with diced green bell peppers for extra freshness.
